====================================================================
3) QUICK LEGEND
====================================================================

- OFFOrchestrator is the central time/causality manager.
- FarmController issues high-level commands per turbine.
- TurbineController converts high-level command + local measurements into actuator setpoints.
- WakeSolver is the bridge between orchestrator and selected WakeModel implementation.
- AtmosphericModel variants are interchangeable (currently: HomogeneousFlow, unsteadyBackgroundFlow).
- Utils is a shared service layer (types, states, coordinates, io, logging).
